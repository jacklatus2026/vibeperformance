Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is VIBE nicotine-free?

Yes — completely. VIBE pouches contain functional actives in a plant-fibre pouch. There's no nicotine, nothing to combust and nothing to inhale.

How long do I keep a pouch in?

Place one under your upper lip for 20–30 minutes. The low-swell fibre system is designed to stay comfortable and discreet for the full session.

When will I feel it?

Most people notice a clean lift within about 10 minutes as the actives cross the gum line, building to a 1–2 hour peak with no spike-and-crash.

How much caffeine is in each one?

VIBE PRO has 75 mg and ENDURE has a 30 mg micro-dose. FOCUS is caffeine-free (driven by 100 mg paraxanthine) and CALM is completely caffeine-free.

Can I use VIBE every day?

Yes — match the SKU to the moment: PRO and ENDURE for training, FOCUS for deep work, CALM for recovery. Full ingredient doses are on the Tech specs page.

Who shouldn't use it?

VIBE is for healthy adults 18+. It is not suitable if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. If you take medication or have a medical condition, consult a healthcare professional first. Not a medicine; keep out of reach of children.
